The Story

Post-War Birmingham.
One Family's Ambition.

Britain, 1919. The Great War has ended, but its scars run deep. Returning soldiers, revolutionary fervour and criminal enterprises jostle for power in a nation torn apart by economic collapse and social upheaval.

At the centre of it all: Thomas Shelby. War hero turned gang lord, a man whose razor-sharp mind and cold-blooded resolve drive his family from the back streets of Small Heath toward the corridors of Westminster itself.

But power invites enemies — and Inspector Chester Campbell has arrived from Belfast with a singular mission: to destroy everything the Shelbys have built.
